No bed in Hospital

Every day many patient died just for not getting bed in Hospital within right time. Patient families runs from one hospital to another hospital to admit the patient and hospitals replies We don't have no more bed. Every moment of time is important for a patient, if there is delay to provide treatment to patient, that means the patient death is certain. Everyday in everywhere this are happening. Why this will happen? The death of the patients are not a normal death, it is a murder by the Healthcare System.
We should solve the problem. The problem will totally solved when Government focus on this issue. But since long decade any government never focus on that issue seriously.
At the moment we may have a scope to try to dealing with the issue.
To help that victims of the poor healthcare system, we try to provide a platform, where anybody can see which hospital has avaiable bed.
If a person easily can see which hospital of his locality has available bed and also can book a bed in advance, then the person don't need to run in serveral hospitals to admit the patient. The person just need to go to that hospital, in which hospital he/she saw, there are available bed using the platform. It will save the time. And save of time means, more chances of patient's recovery. Here by providing Hospital Searching and Bed Booking Features we try to help the people.

Insufficient Doctor in India

WHO recommended ratio of Doctor and people in a country must be 1:1000. But unfortunately India doesn't reach the ratio till now.
Result of that is death of patient due to not get medical assistance at right time. People those who are live far from hospitals for them it is almost impossible to get medical support immediately. If they try to reach to the hospital to give treatment to the patient, it may cause death of patient; because it make delay in treatment. Specially rural people facing the problem; so in that case one solution is contact to a doctor immediately to get medical assistance.
But most of the people can't contact to a doctor to get a medical help. Because they don't know any doctors, they have no inormation of any doctor. How they will able to connect to a doctor in this case?
The first and important solution is establish Primary Healthcare Center in every villages and provide sufficient doctors to that center.
But what the instant solution of that?
In that case to help the people, we provide a platform to the people, from where they can contact to a doctor anytime anywhere. By that platform we can deliver all the essential information about the doctors. People can find doctors of their locality and cotact to the doctor.

No enough blood in Blood Bank

In the country, blood banks have not sufficient blood. And for a common person it is almost impossible to collect blood within time. A patient need blood imedeately, delay in giving blood to a patient means, certain death of patient.
Instead of that, there are no enough blood in blood banks, even there are no data available about blood donors in the blood banks.
How will we get blood if needed? Will we call to everybody and ask them what is your blood group, can you give blood? It will waste lots of time, and in treatment waste of treatment waste of time is a biggest mistake, it will push the patient to the death.
One solution is provide a platform to the people, in which platform contact information of blood donor will be given. When needed a person can contact to the blood donors. It will help the people. And in this case there are no question of time waste; it will save a lots of time. And saving in time means, donate a good time to the patient.

About

We look forwards a time, when nobody dies due to insufficiency of Healthcare Service In India.

Go Healthy is a website for medical support. Purpose of the website is to deliver all details about Bed and Antivenom availability in all hospitals, Doctors and Blood Donor near you and how to do first aid. This website has four different features. In Check Hospital section user can see the all information about all the Hospital and number of available bed in Hospital and also can book bed for patients and can see which hospital has Antivenom. In Find Doctor section user can see all the information about doctors of their location. And in Find Donor section user can see the all information about blood donors. In First Aid section there are several free videos and photos are available which will provide you a guide about First Aid.
