Why need to register a blood bank?

Our aim is to deliver live blood availability data of all the blood bank in the web portal. To archive that we need a universal database for all the blood banks; hence all the blood bank authority must register the blood bank, so that we will able to collect the data of the blood bank. After successfull registration blood bank authorities can maintain the blood availability records, blood donation and collection records, and also can issue digital blood donation Certificates to the blood donors.
We are building a system by that we will collect the data of all the reported blood donations and blood collections, and from that data we can analysis the data of the healthcare system like how many people donates blood in blood bank, how many people receives blood from blood bank. and from that data we will able to examine the scenario of the healthcare system. So to collect these data from your blood bank we need your blood bank's information through registration.

How to register?

  1. Enter all the data of the blood bank by fill up the form.
  2. After fill up all the input fields press the "Next" button.
  3. Now you have to provide the current blood availability details of your blood Bank.
  4. In the final step we will verify your given information and if we find everything is okay in your registration details then we will active your account. After activation of the account you can login to get access.

What registered blood bank can do?

  • Maintain Blood availability Data: Blood Bank authorites can edit blood availability data anytime in the web portal
  • Add and view Blood Donation or Collection Records: Blood bank authorites can add a blood donor's information if he/she donated blood in blood bank, and also can see the information of the added donors. And also can add a patient's information if blood has collected for the patient from blood bank.
  • Issue Blood Donation Certificate: Authority can isssue a digital Blood Donation Certificate to a blood donor if the donor donated blood to the blood bank.
