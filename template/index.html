Our Vision

We look forward a time, when nobody will die due to insufficiency of Healthcare Services In India..

We all know what's the situation of healthcare system in India. Healthcare system facing shortage of beds, medical oxygen, hospitals, doctors. But since a long decades the State never response to fix the problems. And result of that is painful death. A strong and effective action should be taken to make a better healthcare system.
Go Healthy is a website for medical support, we want to help the victims of that poor healthcare system. Purpose of the website is to deliver all details about Bed and Antivenom availability in all hospitals, Doctors and Blood Donor near you and how to do first aid. This website has four different features. In Check Hospital section user can see the all information about all the Hospital and number of available bed in Hospital and also can book bed for patients and can see which hospital has Antivenom. In Find Doctor section user can see all the information about doctors of their location. And in Find Donor section user can see the all information about blood donors. In First Aid section there are several free videos and photos are available which will provide you a guide about First Aid.

Insufficient Doctor in India

WHO recommended ratio of Doctor and people in a country must be 1:1000. But unfortunately India doesn't reach the ratio till now.
Result of that is death of patient due to not get medical assistance at the right time. People those who are live far from hospitals or living in rural area for them it is almost impossible to reach to hospital within time and get medical support immediately, because of lack of rural transport infrastructure. In rural hospital, there are no enough doctors. If patient's family try to reach to a good hospital to give treatment to the patient, it may cause death of patient; because it make delay in treatment. Specially rural people facing the problem; so in that case one solution is contact to a doctor immediately to get medical assistance.
But most of the people can't contact to a doctor to get a medical help. Because they don't know any doctors, they have no information of any doctor. How they will able to connect to a doctor in this case?
The first and important and best solution is establish Primary Healthcare Center in every villages and provide sufficient doctors to that center. But it is the responsibility of the State to do that.
But what the instant solution of that?
In that case to help the people, we provide a platform to the people, from where they can contact to a doctor anytime anywhere. By that platform we can deliver all the essential information about the doctors. People can find doctors of their locality and contact to the doctor.

No enough blood in Blood Bank

In the country, blood banks have not sufficient blood. India facing huge blood crisis. And for a common person it is almost impossible to collect blood within time. A patient need blood immediately, delay in giving blood to a patient means, certain death of patient.
Even blood banks has no information about the blood donors. Most of the times, the hospital authorities put the responsibility of collecting blood on the neck of patient's family. But this is not fair. Hospital authorities never want to take the responsibility of collection blood on their neck.
How will we get blood if needed? Will we call to everybody and ask them what is your blood group, can you donate blood? It will waste lots of time, and in treatment waste of time is the biggest mistake, it will push the patient to the death.
One solution is provide a platform to the people, in which platform contact information of blood donor will be given. And also by that platform, we can collect the data of all volunteer blood donor in a single database. When needed a person can contact to the blood donors. It will help the people. And in this case there are no question of time waste; it will save a lots of time. And saving in time means, donate a good time to the patient.
India has large number of eligible blood donors. If one percent of that be a blood donor, then it could solve that crisis.

Bed availability of India is only 5 beds for 10,000 people.

In Human Development Report 2020, India ranked 155 out of 167 countries on Bed Availability.
That's why many patients dies just for not getting bed in hospital.

India has only 69,265 Hospitals.

Just 25,778 Government Hospitals and 43,487 Private Hospitals.
People living in rural area are facing the problem of lower number of hospitals.

WHO's recommended ratio of doctor and people for a country is 1:1000. That is in a country 1 doctor is required for every 1000 people.

But according to Human Development Index 2020, India has just 8.6 doctors for every 10,000 population, i.e. in India there is 1 doctor for every 1163 people.
As a result, many patient do not receive treatment within in right time. Sometime that causes death of patients.

India facing blood shortage of almost 12 lakh unit.

India has more than 512 million eligible blood donors. This number is enough to fulfill the shortage. WHO recommends that 1 percent of a country’s population would suffice for its blood needs.
So we need more voluntary blood donations. And annually 10 million blood are being collected. Due to lack of knowledge and myths most Indians are not want to donate blood.
Also many bloods are being waste in blood bank before expire date because of lack of storage infrastructure.

In india every day more than 1000 road crashes are being happened and in every day almost 400 people are being died in road accident.

Cause of some of the death is, due to heavy traffic and no emergency lane in road, ambulance or medical emergency team can't reach to the accident spot within the time.
